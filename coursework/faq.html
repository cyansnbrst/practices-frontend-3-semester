Does the LLS offer self-study online courses?

Yes, we offer students a flexible, low-cost self-study course for you to learn language online for life and work. Study languages online in your own time and at your own speed

Does the LLS offer live online classes?

Yes, we offer students a flexible and totally customised online course, with a variety of classes and teachers to choose from. You can customise your study plan to suit your own preferences and study goals

Does the LLS offer personal online tutoring?

Yes, we offer students a personal tutoring service for one-to-one online language tuition with our approved tutors. Our highly qualified tutors will develop a study plan with you focused on achieving your goals

What learning resources are available and how do I use them?

Visit our main page to find out more about the resources and how to use them

Can I use LLS for free?

Yes, you can access some the resources for free. You can also register for a free account to join our community

How do I log in or register for an account?

Go to the log in page. You need your username or email address and your password. If you have forgotten your username or password then visit the reset your password page for help

If you don't already have an account, set up your free account today and get started

How can I check my language level?

Our online level test will give you an approximate indication of your language proficiency level

You can use the result to help you find free resources on our website that are appropriate for your language ability

How do I know what to study?

On LearnEnglish you choose what to study from our free resources. If you need help and support with your English studies we recommend our live online classes where our highly qualified and experienced British Council English teaching professionals can guide you. Our teachers are experienced in working with students from many different countries and are online teaching specialists

How can I improve my speaking and pronunciation?

On LLS you can watch our series of videos in our speaking skills section to practise your speaking skills. You can improve your speaking by noticing the language we use in different situations and practising useful phrases

However, to really put your speaking into practice you need to speak to other learners and ideally to a teacher who can correct your mistakes and advise you on how to improve your speaking and pronunciation. Our live online classes offer exposure to different accents which is essential in a world where language is spoken by millions of people from different countries. We think this is the best way for our students to be better prepared to deal with real-life situations

How can I improve my writing?

On LLS you can find activities to practise your writing skills. You can improve your writing by understanding model texts and how they're structured. Join our online community and write comments and respond to other learners to put your knowledge into practice. Registering for an account is free. Set up your account today and get started

If you need specialised help with your writing, you can book private online classes with our expert teachers
